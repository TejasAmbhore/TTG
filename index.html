ABOUT US

TTG is the official Student body of Sponsored Research and Industrial Consultancy (SRIC), IIT Kharagpur.
We are a body promoting research among the students and acting as a bridge in connecting industrialists, entrepreneurs and researchers.
This is done through workshops such as IPR Workshop and also the Annual Research Meet,
which was started since 2020 itself and have gathered quite some active participation.
